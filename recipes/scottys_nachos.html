Scotty's Nachos

The following is a recipe for cheesy nachos

This is a dish I like to make as a super easy dinner when I get home from work in the afternoon. My mom had so many dishes like this for us growing up and this is turning out to be one of my own.

Ingredients

  • Thin Mission tortilla strips
  • Shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • Your favorite hot sauce

Steps

  1. Place desired amount of tortilla chips on a plate with desired amount of Mexican cheese blend on top
  2. Heat in microwave oven for 1:45min or for other disired amount of time
  3. Top with your favorite hot sauce and enjoy!
