Caprese Burger

The following is the recipe for the caprese burger

This recipe is one of the first dishes my girlfriend and I made together at the beginning of the COVID-19 pandemic. It has always been a special one to me and I hope you enjoy as well!

Ingredients

  • Brioce Buns
  • 1lb of raw ground beef
  • One vine ripe tomato
  • Basil Pesto
  • Fresh Mozzarella

    • Steps

    1. Cut the pound of raw ground beef into four equal parts and roll each into a patty size ball
    2. Slice the vine ripe tomato into four slices
    3. Place each burger on a heated cast-iorn skillet and cook on medium heat, flipping appropriately until paties are 165 degrees
    4. Toast brioce buns if preferable
    5. Build each burger with the cooked patty, a slice of tomato, a slice of fresh mozzarella, and your chosen amount of basil pesto
    6. Serve and enjoy!
