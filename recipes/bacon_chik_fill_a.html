Bacon Chik Fill A

The follwing is a recipe for chicken breasts wrapped in bacon and topped with Chick Fill A sauce

This is a short recipe for a dish I started to make when I discovered that Chick Fill A Sauce being sold in stores.

Ingredients

  • Raw chicken breast tenders
  • Thinly sliced bacon
  • Chik Fill A sauce

Steps

  1. Cook desired number of chcken breast tenders on both sides to a safe temperature of 165 degrees
  2. Cook desired number of bacon slices desired as well
  3. When your chicken tenders and bacon slices are both cooked, wrap bacon slices around the chicken tenders
  4. Top with Chick Fill A sauce and enjoy!
